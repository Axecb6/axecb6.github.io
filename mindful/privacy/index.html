Privacy Policy

Mindful — Screen Time & Focus — Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

Mindful is developed by André Machado. This policy explains how the App handles data. We are committed to your privacy — all data stays on your device.

2. Permissions Used

Usage Access (App Usage Statistics)

Mindful requests permission to read app usage statistics via Android's UsageStatsManager. This data is used solely to display your screen time summary within the App. It is never transmitted off your device and is not shared with any third party.

Accessibility Service

Mindful uses Android's Accessibility Service to detect when a blocked app is opened, allowing the App to display a focus reminder. This service does not read screen content, capture keystrokes, or collect any personal information. It is used only to intercept foreground app transitions.

3. Data Storage

All data — screen time records, focus sessions, settings — is stored locally on your device using Android's standard storage APIs. We do not use any remote databases, cloud storage, or analytics services.

4. No Data Collection

  • No account or registration required.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third party.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Changes

We may update this policy. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
