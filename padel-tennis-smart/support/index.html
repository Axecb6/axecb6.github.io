How to use the app

  • Open the app on your Apple Watch and choose Padel or Tennis.
  • For padel, pick Competition (best of 3 sets) or Leisure (finish after set 1). For tennis, pick Best of 3 or Best of 5 sets.
  • Tap a team's side of the scoreboard to add a point — the app handles games, sets and tie-breaks for you.
  • Use the microphone button to add points by voice instead of tapping.
  • With your permission, the app shows your live heart rate during the match and saves the session as a workout in Apple Health.
  • Finished matches are saved to History, where you can review past results.

Premium

Premium is a one-time purchase (no subscription) that unlocks your full match history.

If you've already purchased Premium (e.g. after reinstalling the app or on a new device), open the paywall and tap Restore Purchase to re-activate it — no extra charge.

Voice input not working?

Voice scoring uses your watch's microphone and speech recognition. Make sure microphone and speech recognition access are allowed for the app in Settings. Manual (tap) scoring always works without this permission.

Heart rate or Health not showing?

Heart rate and workout saving use Apple Health. Make sure you allowed the app's Health access when prompted — you can review or change it in the Watch app or Health settings. Scoring works fully even without Health access.
