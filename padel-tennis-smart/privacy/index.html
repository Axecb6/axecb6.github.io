Privacy Policy

Padel Tennis Smart — Last updated: July 2026

1. Overview

Padel Tennis Smart is an Apple Watch app developed by André Machado for scoring padel and tennis matches. This page explains what data the App handles and how. The App does not require an account.

2. Match History

The App stores your match history (scores, players, dates, duration) locally on your Apple Watch only. This data is never transmitted anywhere by default. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes it.

3. Health & Heart Rate (HealthKit)

With your permission, the App reads your heart rate from Apple Health during a match to show live stats, and can save the match as a workout in Apple Health (including duration and heart rate). This information is handled by Apple's HealthKit and stays on your device and in your iCloud Health data under Apple's control — the App does not send it to any external server or third party. You can grant or revoke Health access at any time in the Watch/Health settings, and the App still works for scoring without it.

4. Voice Commands (optional)

The App lets you record points using voice commands, using your device's microphone and the operating system's speech recognition service (Apple). Audio is processed by the OS to convert speech to text — the App does not record, store, or send audio to any external server. This permission is optional; manual score entry works without it.

5. Multiplayer Rooms (optional)

If you choose to use the multiplayer feature, devices in the same match room synchronise the live score through a Google Firebase Realtime Database. The only data exchanged is the match state (scores), short team labels (such as "T1" / "T2"), an anonymous per-device identifier, and timestamps used to keep the room in sync. No real names, contacts, location, or health data are sent. Room data is temporary and used only to mirror the score between devices during a match. This feature is entirely optional — the App fully works offline for solo scoring.

6. In-App Purchases

The Premium feature is purchased and processed directly by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. The App verifies your entitlement locally via Apple's transaction records and has no access to payment details (card numbers, etc.), which are handled entirely by Apple. The App does not use any third-party purchase or subscription manager.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store — payment processing and purchase verification
  • Apple HealthKit — heart rate and workout data (on-device)
  • Google Firebase — optional multiplayer score sync

The App does not use advertising, analytics, or behavioral tracking.

8. Your Choices

Since match history and health data stay on your device, you can delete them at any time by clearing the App's data, revoking Health access, or uninstalling the App. For purchase-related data held by Apple, requests can be made through Apple's support channels.

9. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
