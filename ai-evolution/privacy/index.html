Privacy Policy

AI Evolution — Last updated: July 2026

1. Overview

AI Evolution ("the App") is developed by André Machado. This policy explains what data the App handles and how. We are committed to protecting your privacy and collecting only what is strictly necessary for the App to work.

2. Data We Collect

  • Anonymous account — when you first open the App, an anonymous identifier is created via Firebase Authentication (Google). No name, email, phone number or other personal information is requested or stored.
  • Game progress — your virtual AI's name, level, skills and game statistics are stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore, linked only to your anonymous identifier.
  • No tracking — the App does not use advertising identifiers and does not track you across other apps or websites.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — anonymous authentication and progress storage. See Firebase Privacy.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier and purchase state, never payment details. See RevenueCat Privacy.
  • Apple App Store — payment processing for subscriptions. We never receive or store your payment information.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Your progress is retained while your anonymous account exists. Deleting the App removes the anonymous credential from your device; the associated progress data becomes permanently inaccessible. To request explicit deletion of stored data, contact us at the email below.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children. The anonymous identifier contains no personal information.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The latest version will always be available at this URL. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
