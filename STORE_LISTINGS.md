## Gringo — Card Game
**Bundle/Package:** com.axecb.gringoGame / com.axecb.gringo_game
**Category:** Card Games / Board Games

**Short description:**
Play the Gringo card game with friends over WiFi — no account needed.

**Full description:**
Gringo is the fun, fast-paced card game — now on your phone.

Connect with friends on the same WiFi and play in real time. Each player sees their own hand on their own device. Memorise your cards, use special abilities, and be the first to declare "Gringo!" with the lowest score.

**Features:**
• Real-time multiplayer over local WiFi (iOS ↔ Android)
• Automatic room discovery — no typing IPs
• Special card abilities: Queen (peek), Jack (blind swap), King (peek & swap)
• Double deck for 4+ players
• Quick Play reactions — match the discard pile instantly

**How to play:**
Each player gets 4 face-down cards. Peek at 2 to memorise them. On your turn, draw from the deck or the discard pile, then swap or discard. When you think you have the lowest score, declare "Gringo!" — others get one last turn. Lowest total wins.

**Keywords:** gringo, card game, multiplayer, wifi game, family game, cabo, board game
