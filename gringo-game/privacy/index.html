Privacy Policy

Gringo — Card Game — Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

Gringo is developed by André Machado. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

2. Local Network

Gringo uses your device's local WiFi network to connect players on the same network for multiplayer games. No data leaves your local network — all game communication happens directly between devices. We do not use any servers or cloud infrastructure.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, location).
  • No analytics or tracking.
  • No advertising SDKs.
  • No account or registration required.

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
