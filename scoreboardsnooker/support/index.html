How to use the app

  • Enter player names and choose the match length ("Best of 3, 5, 7, 9 or 11").
  • Tap Break Off to start the frame, then tap the ball colours as they're potted to update the score.
  • Use the microphone button to log potted balls by voice instead of tapping.
  • Use Switch to change which player is at the table, and Undo to correct a mistake.
  • Tap End Frame when a frame is finished — the app moves on to the next frame automatically.
  • Finished matches are saved to Histórico / History, where you can review past results.

Premium

The free version keeps your last 3 matches in History. Premium unlocks unlimited match history and detailed statistics.

If you've already purchased Premium (e.g. after reinstalling the app or on a new device), open History → Premium and tap Restore Purchase to re-activate it — no extra charge.

Voice input not working?

Voice scoring uses your device's microphone and speech recognition. Make sure microphone access is allowed for the app in your device Settings. Manual (tap) scoring always works without this permission.
