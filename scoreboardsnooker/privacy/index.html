Privacy Policy

Snooker Scoreboard — Last updated: June 2026

1. Overview

Snooker Scoreboard is developed by André Machado. This page explains what data the App handles and how.

2. Match History

The App stores your match history (scores, players, dates) locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted anywhere. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes it. No account is required.

3. Voice Recognition (optional)

The App lets you record points using voice commands, using your device's microphone and the operating system's speech recognition service (Apple/Google). Audio is processed by the OS to convert speech to text — the App does not record, store, or send audio to any external server. This permission is optional; manual score entry works without it.

4. In-App Purchases

The Premium feature is processed via the App Store / Google Play and managed through RevenueCat. To validate your purchase, RevenueCat receives an anonymous device identifier and transaction (receipt) data. The App has no access to payment details (card numbers, etc.), which are handled directly by Apple/Google.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store / Google Play — payment processing
  • RevenueCat — purchase management

The App does not use advertising, analytics, or behavioral tracking.

6. Your Choices

Since match history stays on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling it. For purchase-related data held by RevenueCat/Apple/Google, requests can be made through their respective support channels.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
